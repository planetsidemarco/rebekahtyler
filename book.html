Full Tilt: 8 months + 6 countries + 2 boys + 1 mother + loads of baggage = an inspiring story about the search for happiness and not letting adversity stop you from living your dream.

Orphaned from the age of four, Rebekah was plunged into a pit of despair when her beloved grandmother died. Already struggling with raising her two sons as a single mother, she had reached breaking point. She knew things had to change.

So four months later she sold my house, quit her job and took her two young sons, aged 2 and 10, on an eight-month adventure around the world, travelling to Canada, England, France, Spain, Italy and Vietnam. How hard could it be?

FULL TILT is a story about finding one's inner strength; about love, loss, belonging and the search for happiness.

Rebekah Tyler is a writer with a strong, colourful and original story to tell. There's a deprecating self honesty and a lot of personal courage shining through. Time and time again Rebekah does things that make average folks shake their heads.

- Geoff Walker, Edito
